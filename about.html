About Safari Luxe

Your trusted partner for premium wildlife experiences in Sri Lanka

Our Story

Safari Luxe was founded with a passion for showcasing the incredible wildlife and natural beauty of Sri Lanka. What started as a small venture has grown into one of the most trusted names in luxury safari tours, combining years of local expertise with world-class service standards.

Our journey began when our founders, experienced wildlife enthusiasts and conservation advocates, recognized the need for premium safari experiences that respect both the environment and the expectations of discerning travelers. We believe that experiencing wildlife should be comfortable, educational, and unforgettable.

Today, Safari Luxe operates across Sri Lanka's most renowned national parks, including Yala, Wilpattu, and Udawalawe. We've built lasting relationships with local communities, park authorities, and conservation organizations, ensuring that our tours contribute positively to wildlife preservation while providing guests with exceptional experiences.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional luxury safari experiences that connect travelers with Sri Lanka's incredible wildlife while promoting conservation and supporting local communities. We strive to exceed expectations through expert guidance, premium comfort, and unforgettable moments in nature.

Our Vision

To become the leading luxury safari tour operator in Sri Lanka, recognized for our commitment to excellence, conservation, and creating transformative wildlife experiences. We envision a future where sustainable tourism and wildlife protection go hand in hand.
